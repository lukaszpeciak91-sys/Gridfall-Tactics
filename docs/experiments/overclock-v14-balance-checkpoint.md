## Identity lock

- Internal gameplay id: `overclock`.
- Runtime faction key: `Overclock`.
- Player-facing EN name: **Project H.E.R.D.** — Hostile Engineered Rural Directive.
- Player-facing PL name: **Program P.A.S.Z.A.** — Program Adaptacyjnej Syntezy Zwierząt Agresywnych.

Project H.E.R.D. / Program P.A.S.Z.A. is an unattended late-1980s state agricultural/military breeding program that continued running after humanity disappeared. It is not a civilization, religion, political faction, robot army, cyberpunk system, or futuristic technology faction. Its products are biological military livestock, and its horror is bureaucratic, agricultural, absurd, and official.

## Acceptance rationale

Overclock v14 is accepted because it sits within the existing faction spread while preserving its intended tempo-disrupt / combat-manipulation identity. Its worst matchup is comparable to the existing Swarm vs Wardens 30.5% pressure point, and its best matchup stays below 65%.

Further attempts to force the Aggro matchup upward either overbuffed the deck or produced dead tech cards. The accepted version should enter production as a first-pass balance checkpoint rather than being pushed into Aggro 2.0.

## Mechanical direction

- Short overload windows.
- Forced engagements.
- Conditioned aggression.
- Temporary ATK manipulation until combat.
- One-combat opposed-offline windows.
- Self-decaying stabilizer pressure.
- Expendable biological specialization.
